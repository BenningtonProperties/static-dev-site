About Bennington Properties

We create a home away from home

We are committed to providing exceptional service to our guests and homeowners with personalized service and staff devoted to getting it just right for you. Whether it’s assisting you with finding the perfect homes for your family reunion or ensuring your home is well cared-for, maintained and cleaned, Bennington Properties doesn’t stop until we get it just right.

Our difference is in the details. From comfy beds to stocked kitchens…our entire team works hard to anticipate and meet your every need, from the moment you visit our website until you return home.

Get the vacation you deserve...guaranteed!

We don’t just talk about service, we back it up with our Vacation You Deserve Guarantee. Because when things go wrong, our family of employees are there to immediately set it right.
