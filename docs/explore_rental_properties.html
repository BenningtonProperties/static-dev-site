Frequently Asked Questions

What all is included with my rental fee?

We guarantee a clean home, 24-hour access to our team to answer your needs, and access to local amenities for free or for a reduced fee.

Can we hold an event (family reunion, etc) in your homes?

Yes, absolutely! We have several families that gather together in our homes year after year as a tradition. We have several homes grouped close together of various capacity to fit all members of your family.

What is the “Recreation Fee”?

The recreation fee helps to pay for many of the amenities and services unique to the area including the SHARC facility, tennis courts, paved bike paths, complimentary bicycles, snow plowing, Sunriver Nature Center, Oregon Observatory, the Sunriver Fitness and Aquatic Center, Sage Springs Club and Spa and High Desert Museum.

What is your pet policy?

We have several homes that are dog-loving. For a small additional fee per night, you may bring your fur-baby with you

What is your cancellation policy and is travel insurance available?

Please read these Vacation Rental Terms and Conditions, which also includes our cancelation policy. We encourage our guests to purchase CSA Travel Insurance at any point monies are at risk, but NO LATER than your final trip payment is due (60 days prior to your arrival). It costs 7.10% of your total rent to protect your vacation investment. A little expense now will protect against a BIG loss later.

Why am I being charged a fee by VRBO.com (HomeAway) or Airbnb.com?

HomeAway is the parent company of the website VRBO.com. In early 2016 they began charging all guests (regardless of who they book through) a “booking fee” for use of their website. VRBO.com, along with Airbnb.com (who also charges a “booking fee”) are the largest sources of vacation rentals on the internet.

Bennington Properties pays VRBO.com and Airbnb.com a fee to list all our vacation rentals on their website. To use these websites to list our homes we have to agree to their terms and conditions which include allowing them to charge you a “booking fee.” We have no control over the amount of the fee and we do not see a single penny from this fee. When you book directly from VRBO.com and Airbnb.com you are agreeing to pay this fee to them and it is often hidden within the grand total you are quoted. When we charge you our portion of the rent you will notice that it is LESS than what you were quoted by VRBO.com or Airbnb.com. You should have received an email from VRBO or Airbnb shortly after you booked. This email includes a breakdown of all charges, including their booking fee..

We wouldn't advertise our vacation rentals on these websites if we didn't have to, but so many people use VRBO.com and Airbnb.com to find their vacation homes it has become a critical resource for our business. Essentially you can see this “booking fee” as the price you pay for the convenience of using these websites.

It always pays to book direct. If you plan to travel to Sunriver again in the future (and why would you not?), we recommend you come directly to our website or call one of our very friendly and helpful LOCAL vacation planners. Not only will you get the help you need in finding that perfect vacation home, but you will actually be paying less..

What does your cleaning fee entail? Is it refundable?

We charge a non-refundable cleaning fee so our guests can just relax and enjoy their vacation. We only ask our guests to put all the dishes in the dishwasher and start it before they depart. That is it! Our team will take care of all the rest. Our cleaning fee is backed by our Housekeeping Guarantee.

Will there be internet and cable (or satellite)?

All homes are equipped with cable and free wifi
